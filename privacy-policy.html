The app does not collect any personal data. There is no registration or login functionality. No user content is stored or processed on any servers.

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect certain device-related and usage information, including:

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by the app developer. For more information, see Google’s privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads in your Android device settings:
Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Since no personal data is collected by the developer, user rights under GDPR (e.g., data access, deletion) do not apply. For data collected by Google, please refer to their privacy terms.
